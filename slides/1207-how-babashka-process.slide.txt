BABASHKA/PROCESS

in a terminal:

    $ rlwrap bb
    user> (babashka.process/shell "nvim")

in a REPL:

    (str/split-lines (:out (process/shell {:out :string} "ls")))

See also:

    babashka.process/process

process/process is lower-level than shell.
stdin, stdout arguments and failure mode (errors or returning data)
can be controlled via input arguments.
